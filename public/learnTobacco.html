Learn about the Tobacco Fairy

An old man lived a lonely, solitary life in his tribe. One day a large flock of birds flew down from the Blue Hills nearby. Suddenly a bird fell from the flock from an arrow in its breast, despite no one shooting it. The flock dispersed and the old man investigated the downed bird. There he saw the dead bird turn into a pile of ash, and from that a fairy. The fairy bestowed a gift for the old man: tobacco seeds and a pipe. The old man learns to smoke the leaves and lives the rest of his life dreaming happily.
